# Superstore Profitability Audit: Strategic Analytics Suite

## Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive data analytics audit of the Superstore Giant, a multi-regional retail chain. Using a dataset of over 9,900 transactions, I developed a suite of interactive Tableau dashboards to move beyond surface-level sales metrics and uncover the underlying drivers of profitability.

The goal was to identify profit leaks, evaluate the impact of discounting strategies, and provide actionable recommendations to increase the company's overall profit ratio.

## Key Insights and Results

* **Revenue vs. Profit:** Analyzed 1.90 Million dollars in total sales with a 12.58 percent overall profit ratio.
* **The Discount Trap:** Discovered a critical breaking point at 20 percent discount; any discount exceeding this threshold resulted in consistent losses across all major categories.
* **Regional Leakage:** Identified Texas as a primary concern, yielding a 25,729 dollar loss despite being one of the highest states by sales volume.
* **Product Performance:** Isolated the Tables sub-category as a high-revenue/negative-profit outlier in the East and Central regions.

## Tech Stack and Skills

* **Tool:** Tableau Desktop
* **Techniques:** Parameters, Context Filters, Sets, Calculated Fields, Dashboard Actions, and Dual-Axis Charts.

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Discount Cap:** Implement a hard 20 percent discount limit on low-margin Furniture items.
2. **Market Audit:** Investigate logistics and shipping overheads in Texas and Ohio to address high-volume losses.
3. **Inventory Pivot:** Shift marketing spend from the Tables sub-category to high-performing Technology products like Copiers and Phones.
